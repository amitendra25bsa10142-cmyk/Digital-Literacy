
🛡 Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)
✅ Use strong passwords – Create unique passwords with a mix of
letters, numbers, and symbols for each account.
✅ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) – Add an extra layer of
security to email, social media, and banking apps.
✅ Do not share OTPs or PINs – Banks and apps never ask for
these details.
✅ Verify links before clicking – Avoid suspicious links from
unknown emails, messages, or social media.
✅ Keep apps and devices updated – Regular updates fix security
vulnerabilities.
✅ Be careful on public Wi-Fi – Avoid logging into banking or
personal accounts on unsecured networks.
✅ Limit personal information online – Don’t share address, phone
number, or college ID publicly.
✅ Download apps only from trusted sources – Use official app
stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
💳 UPI / Financial Safety Tips:
✅ Check UPI requests carefully – Remember, you receive money
without entering a PIN; entering a PIN means you are paying.
✅ Do not accept unknown collect requests – Verify the sender
before approving any payment request.
